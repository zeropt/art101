Pseudocoding and Problem-solving

Challenge

We were challenged with producing two sets of pseudocode lists. The first would be a logical list that anyone can follow to complete a specific task. The other described a process for a computer to perform a specific task.

Problems

We were figure out with ideas and stuck on problems

Results

For the the first list, we produced a step-by-step process for changing the display name that shows in a Zoom meeting. 


Setting the Zoom display name:
	Open zoom application or open the zoom site
	Sign in with your zoom account
	If using the app
		Click the settings button (Gear Symbol)
		Click the profile tab
		Click “Edit My Profile”. This will send you to the site
		If not signed in
			Sign in
	If using the site
		Go to Personal via Profile
	On the right-hand side of the page, right of the name field click “Edit”
	Change the text under “Display Name”
	Press save
